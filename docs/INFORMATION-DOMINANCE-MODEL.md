# Information Dominance Model

## Plain-English definition

Information dominance means having the advantage in the information fight. You can gather, protect, understand, and use information better than your adversary, while making it harder for them to do the same.

## Functional model

Information dominance depends on five activities:

1. **Collect** — obtain relevant information  
2. **Protect** — secure your information and systems  
3. **Process** — turn data into usable understanding  
4. **Shape** — influence what others perceive as true  
5. **Deny** — disrupt or degrade the adversary’s information use

## Operational significance

Information dominance improves:

- awareness
- speed of action
- quality of decisions
- coordination
- resilience under pressure

## Teaching shortcut

A very simple class explanation is:

> Know more, protect more, confuse more, decide faster.

## Warning

Information dominance is rarely absolute. It is usually contested, temporary, and highly dependent on timing, context, and credibility.
