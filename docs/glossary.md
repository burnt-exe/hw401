# Glossary

## Fifth domain

A common reference to cyberspace as an operational domain alongside land, sea, air, and space.

## Information superiority

An advantage gained by controlling, protecting, exploiting, and denying relevant information better than an adversary.

## Informational dimension

The layer containing data, content, protocols, and information processing activity.

## Cognitive dimension

The layer involving human perception, trust, judgement, interpretation, and decision-making.

## Decision-loop disruption

Interference with how a target observes, interprets, decides, and acts.

## Influence propagation

The spread of messages or narratives through connected audiences to shape attitudes or behaviour.

## Cyber-physical effect

A digital action that causes a change in physical equipment, industrial systems, or real-world operations.
