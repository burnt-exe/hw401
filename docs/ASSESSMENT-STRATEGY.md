# Assessment Strategy

## Purpose

Assessment in HW401 should verify that learners can interpret cyber operations in context, not just recall terminology.

## Recommended assessment mix

### 1. Knowledge checks

Use short quizzes to validate understanding of:

- the dimensions of cyberspace
- core terminology
- cyber effect categories
- key doctrinal concepts

### 2. Case study analysis

Require learners to analyze a real incident and describe:

- the target
- the operational objective
- the affected dimension or dimensions
- the immediate and downstream effects
- the strategic significance

### 3. Scenario discussion

Use group exercises where learners interpret a hypothetical campaign and identify:

- likely adversary goals
- information dependencies
- decision vulnerabilities
- defensive priorities

### 4. Reflection or short paper

Ask learners to explain how cyber conflict extends beyond networks into information and cognition.

## Rubric priorities

Assess work based on:

- conceptual accuracy
- clarity of operational reasoning
- ability to connect tactics to strategic effect
- use of terminology in correct context
- quality of judgement rather than volume of technical jargon
