# Slide Transcript Strategy

## Goal

Slide transcripts should help instructors speak confidently, not read mechanically.

## Recommended structure per slide

Each transcript block should include:

1. **Instructor talk track** — what to say in natural speech  
2. **Live example** — a quick real-world incident or analogy  
3. **Demo action** — what to open, browse, or show  
4. **Delegate action** — what learners should discuss or answer

## Style principles

- explain the idea before naming the concept
- use short spoken sentences
- avoid reading bullet points word-for-word
- include at least one grounding example per important concept
- end each section with the operational takeaway

## Strong transition line

> The technical action matters, but the strategic value comes from what it changes in the real world.
