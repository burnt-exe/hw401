# Instructor Guide

## Delivery intent

HW401 should be taught as a strategic cyber course, not just a technical malware course. The instructor should continuously connect technical content to operational consequences and decision impact.

## Teaching posture

The instructor should:

- explain jargon in plain language first
- use real incidents to ground abstract concepts
- move from technical event to operational meaning
- keep returning to the physical, informational, and cognitive dimensions
- reinforce that cyber effects are often indirect but still decisive

## Recommended session rhythm

1. Introduce the concept in plain English  
2. Define the formal term  
3. Show a real example  
4. Ask learners what dimension was affected  
5. Connect the incident to strategic effect

## Priority teaching outcomes

Make sure delegates leave able to answer:

- what happened technically
- what changed operationally
- who was affected cognitively
- why the incident mattered beyond IT
