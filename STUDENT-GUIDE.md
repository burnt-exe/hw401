# Student Guide

## What this course is about

HW401 explores how cyber conflict affects technology, information, and human decision-making. It is not only about hacking techniques. It is about understanding why cyber actions matter in military, political, organizational, and societal contexts.

## How to succeed in this course

To do well, learners should:

- listen for the operational meaning of technical actions
- take notes on the three dimensions of cyberspace
- compare incidents using the same analytical structure
- ask what changed in the real world after a cyber event
- connect each concept to a current or historical example

## Recommended note-taking model

For each topic or incident, record:

- what happened technically
- what information changed hands or changed form
- who had to interpret the event
- what decision or behaviour changed as a result

## Reminder

A technically small incident can still have a major strategic or cognitive effect.
