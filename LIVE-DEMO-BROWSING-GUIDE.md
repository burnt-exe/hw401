# Live Demo and Browsing Guide

## Purpose

The live browsing layer makes the course feel current, evidence-based, and operationally real.

## Suggested live demo categories

### Doctrine and strategy

Show official strategy or doctrine pages to ground terminology.

### Threat intelligence and ATT&CK mapping

Use incident writeups and MITRE ATT&CK-style mappings to show how tactics connect to outcomes.

### Infrastructure visibility

Use internet maps, submarine cable visualizations, network outage dashboards, or radar tools to illustrate the physical dimension.

### Information environment

Use current news, public narratives, platform examples, or disinformation trackers to discuss the informational and cognitive dimensions.

## Instructor rules for live browsing

- browse only credible sources
- keep the demo short and purposeful
- narrate what learners should notice
- tie the demo back to the slide immediately
- avoid turning the internet into an unstructured rabbit hole

## Useful question during demos

> What are we looking at here: infrastructure, content, or human interpretation?
