HW401 is a course and content repository focused on **Warfare in the Fifth Domain** — the cyber and information domain where digital infrastructure, influence operations, decision advantage, intelligence, and modern conflict intersect.

This repository is intended to support structured learning, instructional delivery, research, and course material development around cyber warfare, cognitive operations, information dominance, and related security concepts.

---

## Overview

The **fifth domain** is commonly understood as **cyberspace** — alongside land, sea, air, and space.  
This repository is designed to support content, assets, and instructional materials that explore topics such as:

- Cyber warfare fundamentals
- Information operations
- Cognitive and influence operations
- Decision-loop disruption
- Information dominance
- Critical infrastructure and digital battlespace
- Threat actors, tactics, and campaigns
- Strategic, operational, and tactical cyber effects
- Modern conflict in digitally connected environments

## Core Themes

### 1. Information Dominance
Understanding how control over information, visibility, timing, and narrative shapes advantage.

### 2. Decision-Loop Disruption
Exploring how adversaries interfere with perception, orientation, decision-making, and action.

### 3. Influence Propagation
Examining how narratives, persuasion, misinformation, and psychological effects spread across digital ecosystems.

### 4. Cyber as an Operational Domain
Understanding cyber operations as a strategic and operational battlespace.

### 5. Cognitive Domain Effects
Looking at how digital conflict affects belief, trust, perception, morale, and human behavior.
