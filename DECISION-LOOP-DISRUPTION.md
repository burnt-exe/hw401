# Decision-Loop Disruption

## Core idea

Decision-loop disruption means interfering with how an adversary notices events, interprets them, makes choices, and acts.

## Simple teaching model

The most common model is the OODA loop:

- **Observe**
- **Orient**
- **Decide**
- **Act**

## How disruption happens

A cyber or information operation can:

- deny visibility
- flood systems with noise
- manipulate what is seen as true
- create uncertainty or conflicting indicators
- slow communications
- trigger overreaction or paralysis

## Outcomes

Disruption can produce:

- hesitation
- confusion
- poor prioritization
- slower response
- increased friction
- operational mistakes

## Simple explanation for class

> If I can make you see the wrong picture, or see the right picture too late, I can damage your decisions without firing a shot.
